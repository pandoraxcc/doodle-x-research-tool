Network discovery

Discover devices on your network with the IP addresses. Get the information by providing the ip adress / domain name of the network. Usually the home network includes 256 possible adresses, that's equivalent to 192.168.1.0/24 netmask. Please note, if you are connected to the same network, your ip adress won't be in the discovery list.

It is suggested that you use the scanner on your personal devices or network.
